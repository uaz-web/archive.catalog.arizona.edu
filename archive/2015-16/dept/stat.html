The Statistics GIDP is administered by the Graduate Interdisciplinary Programs Office.

The Program in Statistics at the University of Arizona focuses and enhances statistical training and research across the UA campus.  It administers both the M.S. and Ph.D. degrees, as well as a Ph.D. Minor, in Statistics.  The educational goal of the Program is to produce researchers and practitioners who will work at the forefronts of scientific study, and in which modern statistical research is developed and put directly into practice.  Interested individuals should consult with a Program advisor to help choose the options, minor, and/or coursework that best prepares for a career in interdisciplinary statistics.

For more information, contact the Program's Office.
