ART 396A -- Artists' Books and the Use of Visual Narrative  (3 units)
Description:  The objective of the class is to try to re-think our conventional concepts of what makes a book, both in terms of narrative structure and physical form. By looking at something as familiar as the ubiquitous codex book in totally new ways, the course will help students learn to approach all multi-page projects from fresh and new angles. We will investigate various contemporary artists books in our search to find new approaches of expression in the medium of the book form. The class urges students to try many different structural forms and text/image formats, to be playful, to stay loose, to work hard and not be lazy about pushing it further.
