Undecided Students,
No Major Declared, and No Major Selected

Contact your college dean's office for information about academic advising for undecided students in your college.

Students must declare a major when they have earned 55 units or have attained junior standing.  Incoming transfer students with a minimum of 55 transferable units have one semester at the University before they need to declare a major.

If you want help choosing a major or a career, contact one of the offices listed below for guidance.  These offices are staffed by professionals who specialize in helping students identify academic and long-term career goals.

University College
Integrated Learning Center (ILC) 103

University College is the academic home to "undecided" or "exploratory" students at the University of Arizona.  Academic advising services as well as help and resources for students still exploring majors is provided. 

Career Services
Old Main, Lower Level
Phone: 621-2588

Career Services offers a variety of programs which assist students and alumni to develop and implement career plans, gain work-related experience, seek part-time work and gain professional employment after graduation. Their programs include special programs for undecided students.
