Summer 2001 Course Descriptions
Key to Course Descriptions

Courses offered in Summer 2001 are linked to the Schedule of Classes.

All courses listed below are approved to be taught as of  Summer 2001; however, some may not be offered this Summer.  The courses that ARE offered this Summer, in either Pre-Session, Summer I or Summer II, are linked to the Schedule of Classes.  The complete list below is a good indicator of what may be offered over the next few years (contact department about future offerings).

Italian (ITAL)  Dept Info - College Info

ITAL 101 -- Elementary Italian I (4) CDT Listening, speaking, reading, and writing; introduction to the basic structures and vocabulary of Italian. (Does not count toward the Italian major or minor.)

ITAL 102 -- Elementary Italian II (4) I II CDT Listening, speaking, reading and writing; an introduction to the basic structures and vocabulary of Italian, continuation. (Does not count toward the Italian major or minor.) P, ITAL 101 or placement.

ITAL 102Z -- Intensive Elementary Italian (4) I S Contact department for a description of this course. P, language major or demonstrated language proficiency.

ITAL 199 -- Independent Study (1-4) [Rpt.] Contact department for a description of this course.

ITAL 201 -- Intermediate Italian I (4) I II CDT Continued skill development; reinforcement of basic language skills. P, ITAL 102 or placement.

ITAL 202 -- Intermediate Italian II (4) I II CDT Continued skill development; reinforcement of basic language skills. P, ITAL 201 or placement.

ITAL 202Z -- Intensive Intermediate Italian (4) II S Contact department for a description of this course. P, language major or demonstrated language proficiency.

ITAL 230A -- Italian Culture: Middle Ages and Renaissance (3) I Italian culture from the Middle Ages and the Renaissance. P, two courses from Tier One, Traditions and Cultures (TRAD 101,102,103,104). Approved as Tier Two - Humanities.

ITAL 230B -- Italian Culture: Baroque Age to the Present (3) II Italian Culture: Baroque Age to the Present. Required for the major or minor in Italian Studies option. Taught in English. Approved as Tier Two - Humanities.

ITAL 240 -- Italian Folklore and Popular Culture (3) I S A study of the oral narratives (fairy tales, legends, saints' legends) and customary crafts of ordinary Italian and Italian Americans. Approved as Tier Two - Humanities.

ITAL 250A -- Italian Literature in Translation: The Middle Ages (3) I II Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies. Taught in English. P, two courses from Tier One, Traditions and Cultures (TRAD 101,102,103,104).

ITAL 250B -- Italian Literature in Translation: The Renaissance (3) I II Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies. Taught in English. P, two courses from Tier One, Traditions and Cultures (TRAD 101,102,103,104). Approved as Tier Two - Humanities.

ITAL 250C -- Italian Literature in Translation: Italian Theater (3) I II Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies. Taught in English. P, two courses from Tier One, Traditions and Cultures (TRAD 101,102,103,104). Approved as Tier Two - Humanities.

ITAL 250D -- Italian Literature in Translation: The Novel (3) I II Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies. Taught in English. P, two courses from Tier One, Traditions and Cultures (TRAD 101,102,103,104). Approved as Tier Two - Humanities.

ITAL 299H -- Honors Independent Study (1-3) [Rpt.] I Contact department for a description of this course.

ITAL 305A -- Advanced Italian: Composition and Conversation (3) I Emphasis on improving listening, comprehension, speaking and writing. Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies. Taught in English. P, ITAL 202 or consult department before enrolling. Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies. Taught in Italian.

ITAL 305B -- Advanced Italian: Advanced Composition and Conversation (3) II Emphasis on improving listening, comprehension, speaking and writing. Taught in Italian. P, ITAL 202 or consult department before enrolling. Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies.

ITAL 305C -- Advanced Italian: Business Italian (3) I II Emphasis on improving listening, comprehension, speaking and writing. Taught in Italian. P, ITAL 202 or consult department before enrolling. Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies.

ITAL 330A -- Italian Studies: Fascism, Resistance and Reconstruction (3) I II Comprehensive study of a particular aspect of Italian culture: Fascism, Resistance and Reconstruction. Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies. Taught in English.

ITAL 330B -- Italian Americana in Fiction and Film: Crossing Oceans (3) I II Comprehensive study of a particular aspect of Italian culture: Italian Americana in Fiction and Film: Crossing Oceans. Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies. Taught in English. P, two courses from Tier One, Traditions and Cultures (TRAD 101,102,103,104). Approved as Tier Two - Humanities. Fulfills the Gender, Race, Class, Ethnicity, or Non-Western area study requirement.

ITAL 330C -- Italian Studies: Italian Cinema and Literature (3) I Comprehensive study of a particular aspect of Italian culture: Italian Cinema and Literature. Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies. Taught in English.

ITAL 330D -- Women in Italian Society (3) II Comprehensive study of a particular aspect of Italian culture: Women in Italian Society. Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies. Taught in English. P, two courses from Tier One, Individuals and Societies (INDV 101, 102, 103, 104). Approved as Tier Two - Individuals and Societies. Fulfills the Gender, Race, Class, Ethnicity, or Non-Western area study requirement.

ITAL 399 -- Independent Study (1-4) [Rpt.] Contact department for a description of this course.

ITAL 399H -- Honors Independent Study (1-3) [Rpt.] I II Contact department for a description of this course.

ITAL 400A -- Main Currents of Italian Literature: The Middle Ages and Renaissance (3) I Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies. Taught in Italian. P, ITAL 202 or consult department before enrolling. May be convened with ITAL 500A.

ITAL 400B -- Main Currents of Italian Literature: The 17th through 20th Centuries (3) II Counts toward the major or minor in Italian or Italian Studies. Taught in Italian. P, ITAL 202 or consult department before enrolling. May be convened with ITAL 500B.
