FSHD 450 -- Human Sexuality and Relations (3) Overview of human sexuality from development and interpersonal perspectives, topics to include reproductive physiology and health, sexual function and dysfunction, educational and intervention strategies.

FSHD 477 -- Genetic Basis of Normal and Deviant Traits (3) II Explores methods of studying genetic influences on human traits and summarizes research findings on normal traits, such as sociability and IQ, and on deviant traits such as criminality.
