Spring 2001 Course Descriptions
Key to Course Descriptions

NEW Feature:  Only courses offered in Spring 2001 link to the Schedule of Classes.

All courses listed below are approved to be taught as of  Spring 2001; however, some may not be offered this term.  Only courses offered this term are linked to the Schedule of Classes.  The complete list below is a good indicator of what may be offered over the next few years (contact department about future offerings).

Critical Languages (CRL )  Dept Info - College Info

CRL 101 -- Elementary Language Study (4) [Rpt./ 1] Introduction to the language with an emphasis on its spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly tutorial reviews. 2R, 6L. Fees. P, course may be repeated if language is different.

CRL 102 -- Elementary Language Study (4) [Rpt./ 1] Continued introduction to the language with an emphasis on its spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly tutorial reviews. Fees. P, CRL 101. Course may be repeated if language is different.

CRL 197A -- Elementary Language Study (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II Contact department for a description of this course. Fees. P, CRL 102 or consent of CLP/language examiner.

CRL 201 -- Intermediate Language Study (4) [Rpt./ 1] Continuing study of the language with emphasis on its spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly reviews. 2R, 6L. Fees. P, CRL 102. Course may be repeated if language is different.

CRL 202 -- Intermediate Language Study (4) [Rpt./ 1] Continuing study of the language with an emphasis on its spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly reviews. 2R, 6L. Fees. P, CRL 201. Course may be repeated if language is different.

CRL 297A -- Intermediate Language Study (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II Contact department for a description of this course. Fees. P, CRL 202 or consent of CLP/language examiner.

CRL 301 -- Advanced Language Study (3) [Rpt./ 1] I II Continuing study of the language with an emphasis on developing reading and writing skills, continuing practice with spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly reviews. 2R, 4L. Fees. P, proficiency at 202 level; course may be repeated if language is different.

CRL 302 -- Advanced Language Study (3) [Rpt./ 1] I II Continuing study of the language with an emphasis on developing reading and writing skills, continuing practice with spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly reviews. 2R, 4L. Fees. P, proficiency at 202 level. Course may be repeated if language is different.

CRL 397A -- Advanced Language Study (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II Contact department for a description of this course. Fees. P, CRL 302 or consent of CLP/language examiner.

CRL 497A -- Specialized Language Study (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II Contact department for a description of this course. Fees. P, CRL 397 or consent of language examiner.

CRL 498 -- Senior Capstone (3-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II Contact department for a description of this course.
