Spring 2001 Course Descriptions
Key to Course Descriptions

NEW Feature:  Only courses offered in Spring 2001 link to the Schedule of Classes.

All courses listed below are approved to be taught as of  Spring 2001; however, some may not be offered this term.  Only courses offered this term are linked to the Schedule of Classes.  The complete list below is a good indicator of what may be offered over the next few years (contact department about future offerings).

Persian (PRS )  Dept Info - College Info. Also see Arabic and Near Eastern Studies under separate listings.

PRS 101 -- Elementary Persian I (5) I II CDT Conversation, reading, and composition in modern Persian.

PRS 102 -- Elementary Persian II (5) I II CDT Conversation, reading, and composition in modern Persian. P, PRS 101.

PRS 349 -- Classical Persian Literature in English (3) II Historical, cultural and literary issues related to the readings will be discussed.

PRS 350 -- Contemporary Persian Literature in English (3) II Historical, cultural and literary issues related to the readings will be discussed.

PRS 401 -- Intermediate Persian I (5) I II CDT Conversation in the dialect of contemporary Iran; extensive readings in classical and modern literature. P, PRS 102.

PRS 402 -- Intermediate Persian II (5) I II CDT Conversation in the dialect of contemporary Iran; extensive readings in classical and modern literature. P, PRS 401.

PRS 403 -- Advanced Persian I (3) [Rpt./ 8 units] I II CDT Readings in Persian, with the objective of preparing the student for independent research. P, PRS 402. May be convened with PRS 503.

PRS 404 -- Advanced Persian II (3) [Rpt./ 1] I II CDT Readings in Persian, with the objective of preparing the student for independent research. P, PRS 403. May be convened with PRS 504.

PRS 498 -- Senior Capstone (1-3) I II Contact department for a description of this course.

PRS 499 -- Independent Study (1-6) [Rpt.] I II Contact department for a description of this course.

PRS 503 -- Advanced Persian I (3) [Rpt./ 8 units] I II CDT For a description of course topics see PRS 403. Graduate-level requirements include additional readings and translations. May be convened with PRS 403.

PRS 504 -- Advanced Persian II (3) [Rpt./ 1] I II CDT For a description of course topics see PRS 404. Graduate-level requirements include additional readings and translations. May be convened with PRS 404.

PRS 599 -- Independent Study (1-6) [Rpt.] I II Contact department for a description of this course.
