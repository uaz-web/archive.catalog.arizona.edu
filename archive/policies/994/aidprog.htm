Academic Progress to Maintain Scholarships and Financial Aid

To maintain scholarships and financial aid, students must:

  • Maintain satisfactory academic progress as defined by The University of Arizona.
  • Comply with all the terms specified on the back of the Notice of Award and Acceptance Agreement.
  • Complete full-time course loads during fall and spring semesters, unless the Office of Student Financial Aid gives prior approval. Since costs for part-time attendance are less, aid offers may have to be adjusted downward or eliminated. Full-time enrollment for undergraduate students is 12 units per semester.
