Academic Policies

Change of Major or College

Students wishing to change major or college must first receive permission from the dean’s office of the college to which they wish to transfer. Change from one college to another is initiated by filing a change of college form with the new college. For information about timetables for changing your major or college, contact the dean of the college to which you wish to change.
