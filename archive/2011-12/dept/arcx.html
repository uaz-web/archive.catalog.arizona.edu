The School:

The School of Architecture is in the College of Architecture and Landscape Architecture. The School offers an accredited five-year program leading to the professional degree, Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch.). A post-professional master's program (M.Arch.) and a two-track program leading to a professional Master of Architecture degree are also offered.

For more information contact the school office listed above.
