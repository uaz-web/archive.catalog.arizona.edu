SOIL, WATER AND ENVIRONMENTAL SCIENCE (SWES)

The Department of Soil, Water and Environmental Science provides students with a variety of programs in environmental science, soil science, water quality and soil-plant-nutrient-water relationships. The department is responsible for the university-wide undergraduate Bachelor of Environmental Science degree.
