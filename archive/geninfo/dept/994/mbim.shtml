Degrees, Majors, Minors and Options

The graduate program in microbiology and immunology offers research opportunities in all major areas of microbiology and immunology. The research systems used include viruses, bacteria, bacterial plasmids, fungi, protozoans, parasites, cell and tissue culture, and animal models used in immunological studies.

For academic requirements for graduate degrees, consult the Graduate Catalog and the departmental office listed above.
