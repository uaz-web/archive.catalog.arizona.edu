FINANCE (FIN)

Majors in finance are prepared for corporate financial management, investment portfolio management, brokerage, and investment and commercial banking.

Undergraduate majors in finance are offered through the Bachelor of Science in Business Nonbusiness students interested in a minor in one of these areas should contact the department head for information. The department participates in the Master of Business Administration and the Doctor of Philosophy degrees with a major in business administration.
