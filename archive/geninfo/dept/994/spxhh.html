SPEECH AND HEARING SCIENCES (SP H)

The Department of Speech and Hearing Sciences prepares students for careers in basic and clinical sciences (speech-language pathology, audiology, speech science, and hearing science) in university, laboratory, medical, public school, or other clinical settings. Professional certification in the state of Arizona and with the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association requires graduate study with the acquisition of a Master of Science degree.

The department offers the following degrees: Bachelor of Science in Speech and Hearing Sciences, Master of Science and Doctor of Philosophy with a major in speech and hearing sciences. For graduate admission and degree requirements, consult the Graduate Catalog.
