INFORMATION RESOURCES AND LIBRARY SCIENCE

(LI S)

The School of Library Science offers courses focusing on the study of information and its use as a social phenomenon. The school offers an undergraduate minor, the Master of Arts degree with a major in library science and the Doctor of Philosophy degree with a major in library science. For admission and degree requirements, please see the Graduate Catalog.
