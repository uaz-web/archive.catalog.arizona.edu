PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION AND POLICY

(PA )

The School of Public Administration and Policy offers two degrees designed to prepare men and women for a variety of staff and managerial positions in public sector and not-for-profit organizations, and private organizations dealing with the public sector. The degrees offered are the following:

The Bachelor of Science in Public Administration with majors in public management, health and human services administration, and criminal justice administration.

The Master of Public Administration. For admission and degree requirements, please see the Graduate Catalog.

The School participates in the Honors program.
