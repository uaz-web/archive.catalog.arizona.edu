Military science (Army), naval science (Navy and Marine Corps) and aerospace studies (Air Force) are open to male and female students seeking a commission. ROTC courses can be counted as elective credit toward graduation in most academic majors. Lower-division courses carry no service commitment. Veterans may receive credit for the first two years of the four-year ROTC program. Textbooks and uniforms are provided by the departments. For further information about the four-year ROTC programs, the special two-year ROTC programs, entry requirements for upper-division courses, and ROTC scholarships, see the on-line catalog or contact the department.
