ATMOSPHERIC SCIENCES

(ATMO)

The Department of Atmospheric Sciences offers the Bachelor of Science degree, with a hydrometeorology option, and the Master of Science and Doctor of Philosophy degrees. For graduate admission and degree requirements, consult the Graduate Catalog.

The department participates in the honors program.

Students should have a good working knowledge of the material covered in the prerequisites that are listed for all ATMO courses.
