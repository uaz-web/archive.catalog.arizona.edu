FINE ARTS

Minimum Requirements for Completion of Bachelor's Degrees

Fine arts students are subject to general university requirements governing completion of bachelor’s degrees. The college also requires students in all B.F.A. and B.M degree programs to complete at least 45 units outside of the major department; these units may include general education requirements.

For more information about graduate degree programs please consult the Graduate Catalog.

General Education Program

All undergraduate students are required to complete a common general education program. Designed to provide a foundation for university learning, the program develops students’ creative and analytical skills and integrates knowledge across university disciplines.
