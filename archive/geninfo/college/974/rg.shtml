The Graduate College administers all University of Arizona graduate degrees. The college publishes the Graduate Catalog which contains information about available graduate programs, admissions and other college policies. For more information about a particular graduate program. consult the Graduate Catalog or the offering department.

To obtain a copy of the Graduate Catalog, contact the college at the above address.
