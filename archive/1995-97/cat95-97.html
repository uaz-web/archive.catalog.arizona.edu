The 1995-97 General Catalog is being presented as an on-line document in conjunction with the UAInfo project. The information contained in this production in most cases is identical to that found in the printed version. However, we have used more current information when it was available. In fact, one of the advantages inherent in this type of document is the ease in making changes and maintaining the most current policies and applicable information.
