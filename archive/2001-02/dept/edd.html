Associate Teaching Majors

Several academic units offer programs for training teachers in their particular disciplines in association with the College of Education.  Students in these programs earn degrees specific to the offering unit.

Major and Degree: Academic Unit:
Agricultural Technology Management & Education (B.S.) College of Agriculture & Life Sciences
Art Education (B.F.A.) College of Fine Arts
Family & Consumer Sciences Education (B.S.) School of Family & Consumer Sciences
Health Education (B.S.H.S.) School of Health Professions
Music Education (B.M.) Instrumental Voice School of Music & Dance
Theatre Arts Education (B.F.A.) College of Fine Arts
