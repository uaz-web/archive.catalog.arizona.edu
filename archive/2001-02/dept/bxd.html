  • Pre-Business

Undergraduate Minors -- minors link to Minor Requirement Reports.

The undergraduate programs do not require a minor; however students may select an optional minor.  Minors available within the college are:

  • Criminal Justice Administration Minor
  • Economics Minor
  • Economics Teaching Minor
  • General Business Administration Minor
  • Health and Human Services Administration Minor
  • Public Management Minor
