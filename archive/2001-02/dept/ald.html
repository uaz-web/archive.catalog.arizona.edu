Undergraduate Minors -- minors link to Minor Requirement Reports.

Completion of a minor is not required for graduation in the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences.  Minors available in the college are:

  • Agricultural and Biosystems Engineering Minor*
  • Agricultural Economics and Management Minor
  • Animal Sciences Minor
  • Environmental and Water Resource Economics Minor
  • Environmental Sciences Minor
  • Family and Consumer Sciences Education Minor
  • Family Studies and Human Development Minor
  • Microbiology Minor
  • Nutritional Science Minor
  • Plant Science Minor
  • Soil and Water Science Minor
  • Veterinary Science Minor
  • Wildlife, Watershed and Rangeland Resources Minor

* Jointly administered with the College of Engineering and Mines
