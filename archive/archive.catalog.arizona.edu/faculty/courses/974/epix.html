610. Biostatistics for Research (3) Descriptive statistics and statistical inference relevant to biomedical research, including data analysis, regression and correlation analysis, analysis of variance, survival analysis, biological assay, statistical methods for epidemiology and statistical evaluation of clinical literature. P, MATH 509.

650. Quantitative Epidemiology (3) study of computer intensive multivariate epidemiologic methods including evaluation of potential etiologic environmental exposures in human populations to the rise of disease. 2R, 1L. P, 596a, 596b.

660. Infectious Disease Epidemiology (3) [Rpt./1] Introduction to epidemiologic methods used in infectious disease investigations. An emphasis will be placed on understanding the relationships between the host, the parasite and the environment as they relate disease causation. P, 596a, 596b. (Identical with ENTO 660, PHL 660, and V SC 660).

670. Chronic Disease Epidemiology (3-4) Nutritional epidemiology, pharmacoepidemiology, occupational epidemiology and environmental epidemiology. P, 596a, 596b.

680. Respiratory and Environmental Epidemiology (3) Epidemiological methods and research in respiratory diseases and environmentally related diseases, with emphasis on adult and childhood chronic lung diseases, effects of air pollution and occupational exposures. P 596a, 596b, PHL 576.
