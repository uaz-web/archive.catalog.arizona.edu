REMOTE SENSING & SPATIAL ANALYSIS (REM)

490. Remote Sensing for the Study of Planet Earth (3) A multidisciplinary course delineating the physical basis of electromagnetic remote sensing, the concepts of information extraction, and applications pertinent to earth systems science. (Identical with ATMO 490, G EN 490, GEOS 490, HWR 490, MN E 490, OPTI 490, RNR 490, SWES 490). May be convened with 590.

590. Remote Sensing for the Study of Planet Earth (3) For a description of course topics see 490. Graduate-level requirements include an additional term paper. (Identical with ARL 590, ATMO 590, G EN 590, GEOS 590, HWR 590, MN E 590, OPTI 590, RNR 590, SWES 590). May be convened with 490.
