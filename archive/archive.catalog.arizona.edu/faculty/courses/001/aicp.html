Arizona International College: Capstone (AICP)  College Info

AICP 498A Capstone Project I (3-12) I II Students will develop and begin to work on their Capstone Project using the tools and methods learned in their Junior Seminar course. The Capstone Project is a thesis or project initiative that is grounded in the areas of study selected by the student and robustly representative of the knowledge, skill, qualities of mind, and judgment acquired by the student in the course of his or her program of studies. P, senior standing at AIC or consent of instructor.

AICP 498B Capstone Project II (3-12) [Rpt./ 1] I II Students will continue work on the Capstone Project that was begun during AICP 498A. The Capstone Project is a thesis or project initiative that is grounded in the areas of study selected by the student and robustly representative of the knowledge, skill, qualities of mind, and judgment acquired by the student in the course of his or her program of studies. P, consent of the instructor.
