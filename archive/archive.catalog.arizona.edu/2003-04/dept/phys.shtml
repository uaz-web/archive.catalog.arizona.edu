The B.S. program requires a minor.  Students may select a minor from the list of approved minors.  Minors available within the department are:

  • Physics Minor
  • Physics Teaching Minor

The Department:

The Department of Physics is in the College of Science.

Undergraduate Research:

The department strongly encourages its majors to become involved in research at the undergraduate level.  Students may obtain academic credit for such research.  In addition, a theoretical astrophysics concentration is available to Physics majors.  This concentration does not lead to a special degree but is instead an informal program of advising and research opportunities.

For more information contact the department listed above.
