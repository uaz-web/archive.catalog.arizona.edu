Graduate College Policy

Graduate students are subject to the university-wide academic policies listed in Desert Lynx. There are additional policies that govern academic progress towards a graduate degree. Graduate students are advised to view the Graduate Catalog, or to contact the Graduate College with any questions regarding a specific Graduate College policy.
