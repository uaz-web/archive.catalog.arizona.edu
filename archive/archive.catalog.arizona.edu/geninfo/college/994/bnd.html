Undergraduate Minors

No minor is required for the undergraduate program. A student may select an optional minor from the list of approved minors. Minors available within the college are:

Criminal Justice Administration

Economics Minor

Economics Teaching Minor

General Business Administration

Health and Human Services Administration

Public Management

Double majors are common within the College of Business and Public Administration as many of the major requirements overlap. Consult an academic advisor for more information.

* Jointly administered with the College of Social and Behavioral Sciences.

** For more information about this major, contact the Undergraduate Programs Office at 621-2505.

*** Concentrations are available in accounting, finance, organization behavior, management information systems, marketing, and public administration.
