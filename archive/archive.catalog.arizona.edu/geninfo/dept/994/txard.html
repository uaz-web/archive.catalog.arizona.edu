Undergraduate Minors

A minor is required in the Bachelor of Arts degree programs. The minor is usually completed in a department related to the major; students are urged to consult with the major advisor. A minor is not required for all B.F.A. and programs. Consult with your major advisor or view the APRR for your major for more information.
