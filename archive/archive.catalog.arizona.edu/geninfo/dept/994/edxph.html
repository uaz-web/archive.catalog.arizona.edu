EDUCATIONAL PSYCHOLOGY (ED P)

The Department of Educational Psychology offers programs leading to the M.A., M.Ed., Ed.S., and Ph.D. degrees. The M.A. program is in Educational Psychology; the M.Ed. is in School Counseling -- pending ABOR approval, please contact department.  The Ph.D. program is in Educational Psychology with concentrations in Teaching, Learning, and Development, or in Measurement and Methodology.
