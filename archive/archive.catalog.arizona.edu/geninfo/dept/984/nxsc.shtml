Nutritional Sciences Courses and Descriptions

Faculty of the Department of Nutritional Sciences

The Department of Nutritional Sciences provides instructional programs in all areas of nutrition and food safety. These programs prepare students for careers in various phases of the governmental regulatory and consumer agencies, health care delivery systems, and for graduate study or professional schools of medicine, dentistry, physical therapy or veterinary medicine.
