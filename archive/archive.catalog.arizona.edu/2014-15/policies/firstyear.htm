First-Year Writing (Composition) Requirement

Students must complete one of three strands depending upon placement.  Placement in composition courses is based on the student’s high school record and standardized test scores, including the ACT1 English or the RSAT1 Verbal standardized test scores.

  1. A two-course (7 credits) sequence in two semesters of:
    • English 101A (4 credits) and English 102
    • English 107A (4 credits) and  English 108 (the ESL equivalents)
  2. A two-course (6 credits) sequence of:
    • English 101 and English 102, OR
    • English 107 and English 108 (the ESL equivalents)
  3. One course (3 credits) with honors placement:
    • English 109H – a grade of C or higher is required to satisfy the requirement with this course.  (Note: a grade of D necessitates a repeat of English 109H or completion of English 102 to fulfill the requirement.)

Transfer students who have at least 56 transfer credits are eligible for the Transfer Portfolio option.  Students must (1) submit the portfolio within two semesters of transferring to the University of Arizona (including summer sessions), and (2) attend a portfolio information session or make an appointment to speak with the Writing Program Transfer and Placement Coordinator.   The Coordinator may be contacted at writingplacement@email.arizona.edu, or at 520-621-3553.

NOTE:  Students who have earned a previous bachelor's or master's degree from an accredited U.S. institution have satisfied the First-Year Writing (Composition) requirement; they do not need to submit a portfolio.   

For more information about the Transfer Portfolio, see the Writing Program Website.
