The College of Architecture, Planning and Landscape Architecture prepares students to participate in the shaping of our built environment through an NAAB accredited five-year program. Organized with the design studio as the element of focus, the program is a meeting place for the arts and sciences. Students investigate both the relationships between human and natural forces and the relationships between materials and technologies.
