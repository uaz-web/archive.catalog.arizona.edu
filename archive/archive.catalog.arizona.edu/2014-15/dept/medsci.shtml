Major and Degrees

  • Medical Sciences, M.S.
  • Medical Sciences, Ph.D.

 

The Medical Sciences Program is administered in the College of Medicine. Participating clinical departments include Surgery, Medicine, and Pediatrics, along with the science departments of Cell Biology and Anatomy, Immunobiology, Physiological Sciences, and Pharmacology.

The Master of Science and Doctor of Philosophy programs in Medical Sciences train medical residents and fellows in medical and basic sciences, scientific writing, experimental design and analytical techniques to become effective physician-scientists.
