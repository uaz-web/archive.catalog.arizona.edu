Undergraduate Minors -- minors link to Minor Requirement Reports.

A minor is required in the B.A. degree programs. The minor is usually completed in a department related to the major; students are urged to consult with the major advisor.  Minors are optional for all B.F.A. and B.M. degree programs. See your APRR.

Minors available within the college are:

  • Art History Minor
  • Dance Minor
  • Media Arts Minor
  • Music Minor
  • Music Education Teaching Minor
  • Studio Art Minor
  • Theatre Arts Minor
  • Theatre Arts Teaching Minor
