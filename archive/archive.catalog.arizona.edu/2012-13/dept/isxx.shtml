The Integrated Science Program is administered by the College of Science.

This program provides talented undergraduates with an opportunity to pursue studies that go beyond the traditional major in a single field of science. Through coursework, research projects, and internships, students majoring in Integrated Science explore the interrelationships among the sciences, the boundaries along which new fields are born, the discoveries made possible by cross-disciplinary research, and the excitement of such research enterprises here at the University of Arizona.
