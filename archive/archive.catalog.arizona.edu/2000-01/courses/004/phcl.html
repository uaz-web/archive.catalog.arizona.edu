PHCL 800 Research (1-6) [Rpt./] I II

PHCL 801 The Pharmacological Basis of Therapeutics (6) [Rpt./] I Actions of chemical agents upon living material at all levels of organization, with emphasis on mechanisms of action of prototype drugs; foundation for a rational approach to human therapeutics and toxicology. P, PSIO 580 or PSIO 601; and course equivalent to BIOC 462A. Available as both PHCL 801 and PCOL 501.
