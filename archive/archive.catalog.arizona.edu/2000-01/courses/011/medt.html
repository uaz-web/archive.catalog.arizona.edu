Spring 2001 Course Descriptions
Key to Course Descriptions

NEW Feature:  Only courses offered in Spring 2001 link to the Schedule of Classes.

All courses listed below are approved to be taught as of  Spring 2001; however, some may not be offered this term.  Only courses offered this term are linked to the Schedule of Classes.  The complete list below is a good indicator of what may be offered over the next few years (contact department about future offerings).

Medical Technology (MEDT)  Dept Info - College Info

MEDT 195A -- Introduction to Clinical Laboratory Medicine (1) II Contact department for a description of this course.

MEDT 387 -- Contemporary Perspectives of the Medical Technology Professions (3) [Rpt./ 1] I History and current social and economic issues facing the profession of medical technology. Effects of recent legislation on laboratory management. P, consult program director before enrolling.

MEDT 393 -- Internship (1-15) [Rpt.] Contact department for a description of this course.

MEDT 471L -- Fundamental Laboratory Techniques in Clinical Hematology (2) [Rpt./ 1] II Basic laboratory techniques in clinical hematology with emphasis on manual and automated hematological procedures. Instruction includes proper procedural methodologies, quality control, the use of controls and standards, and interpretation of laboratory test results. Fees. P, or CR, MEDT 471R, consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 571L.

MEDT 471R -- Lectures in Clinical Hematology (5) [Rpt./ 1] II Lectures in basic hematology and hematological procedures including cell structure and function, inherited and acquired anomalies, hemostasis, cell enumeration and differentiation, cytogenetics. P, consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 571R.

MEDT 472L -- Fundamental Laboratory Techniques in Clinical Immunology and Immunohematology (2) [Rpt./ 1] I Basic laboratory techniques in serological procedures and blood banking. Emphasis will be placed on procedural methodologies, quality control, the use of controls and standards, and interpretation of laboratory test results. Fees. P, MEDT 472R or MEDT 572R, consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 572L.

MEDT 472R -- Lectures in Clinical Immunology and Immunohematology (4) [Rpt./ 1] I Lectures in serological methods used in the clinical laboratory and interpretation of results; blood banking procedures. P, consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 572R.

MEDT 473L -- Fundamental Laboratory Techniques in Clinical Chemistry (2) [Rpt./ 1] II Basic laboratory techniques in clinical chemistry. Emphasis will be placed on procedural methodologies, quality control, the use of controls and standards, and interpretation of laboratory test results. Fees. P, or CR, MEDT 473R or MEDT 573R. Consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 573L.

MEDT 473R -- Lectures in Clinical Chemistry (5) [Rpt./ 1] II Lectures encompassing the fundamental concepts of clinical laboratory chemistry including pathophysiology and clinical correlations. P, consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 573R.

MEDT 474L -- Fundamental Laboratory Techniques in Clinical Bacteriology (2) I Basic laboratory techniques used in the isolation and identification of bacteria pathogenic for humans. Standard and specialized media/biochemical tests are utilized. Fees. P, or CR, MEDT 474R or MEDT consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 574L.

MEDT 474R -- Lectures in Clinical Bacteriology (5) I Lectures relating to laboratory techniques used to safely isolate and identify pathogenic bacteria. Special media/tests, organismal virulence factors, pathological effects occurring within the host and antibiotic susceptibility testing of bacteria are covered. P, consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 574R.

MEDT 475A -- Topics in Clinical Microbiology: Clinical Parasitology (2) [Rpt./ 1] Clinical Parasitology. Diagnostic methodologies with emphasis on the laboratory identification of clinically relevant parasites. P, consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 575A.

MEDT 475B -- Topics in Clinical Microbiology: Clinical Virology (1) [Rpt./ 1] Clinical Virology. Diagnostic methodologies with emphasis on the laboratory identification of clinically relevant viruses. P, consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 575B.

MEDT 475C -- Topics in Clinical Microbiology: Clinical Mycology and Mycobacteriology (1) [Rpt./ 1] Clinical mycology and mycobacteriology. Diagnostic methodologies with emphasis on the laboratory identification of clinically relevant fungi and Mycobacterium sp. P, consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 575C.

MEDT 476 -- Principles of Laboratory Science (3) [Rpt./ 1] II Basic principles of laboratory mathematics, biostatistics, body fluids analysis, urinalysis, quality control and laboratory safety. P, consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 576.

MEDT 481 -- Clinical Laboratory Hematology (4) [Rpt./ 1] II S Clinical laboratory rotation in hematology. P, MEDT387, MEDT471L/571L, MEDT471R/571R, MEDT472R/572R, MEDT472L/572L, MEDT473R/573R, MEDT473L/573L, MEDT474R/574R, MEDT474L/574L, MEDT475A/575A, MEDT475B/575B, MEDT475C/575C, MEDT476/576, MEDT496A. Consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 581.

MEDT 482 -- Clinical Laboratory Immunology and Immunohematology (5) [Rpt./ 1] I II Clinical laboratory rotation in serology and blood banking. P, MEDT387, MEDT471L/571L, MEDT471R/571R, MEDT472R/572R, MEDT472L/572L, MEDT473R/573R, MEDT473L/573L, MEDT474R/574R, MEDT474L/574L, MEDT475A/575A, MEDT475B/575B, MEDT475C/575C, MEDT476/576, MEDT496A. Consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 582.

MEDT 483 -- Clinical Laboratory Chemistry (5) [Rpt./ 1] I II Clinical laboratory rotation in chemistry. P, MEDT387, MEDT471L/571L, MEDT471R/571R, MEDT472R/572R, MEDT472L/572L, MEDT473R/573R, MEDT473L/573L, MEDT474R/574R, MEDT474L/574L, MEDT475A/575A, MEDT475B/575B, MEDT475C/575C, MEDT476/576, MEDT496A. Consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 583.

MEDT 484 -- Clinical Laboratory Microbiology (5) [Rpt./ 1] I II Clinical laboratory rotation in microbiology. P, MEDT387, MEDT471L/571L, MEDT471R/471R, MEDT472R/572R, MEDT472L/572L, MEDT473R/573R, MEDT473L/573L, MEDT474R/574R, MEDT474L/574L, MEDT475A/575A, MEDT475B/575B, MEDT475C/575C, MEDT476/576, MEDT496A. Consult program director before enrolling. May be convened with MEDT 584.
