Major and Degrees -- undergraduate degrees link to the Degree/Academic Program Requirements Reports (APRRs).
  • Astronomy (B.S., M.S., Ph.D.)
Undergraduate Minors -- minors link to Minor Requirements Reports.

A minor is not required, although students may select an optional minor from among the list of approved University minors. The department offers a structured minor intended primarily for students majoring in other sciences:

  • Astronomy Minor

Prospective Students:

Prospective students should be aware that the curriculum for this major is very demanding, and requires solid preparation in high school mathematics and science. A student who lacks such preparation should expect to take longer than four years to complete the degree requirements.

For more information contact the department office listed above.
