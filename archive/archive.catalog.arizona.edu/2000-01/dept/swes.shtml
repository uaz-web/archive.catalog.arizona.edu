The Department of Soil, Water, and Environmental Science is in the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences.

Degrees, Majors, Minors, and Options:

Baccalaureate Degree
  • Bachelor of Science (B.S.)
  • Bachelor of Science in Environmental Sciences (B.E.S.)
Graduate Degrees -- consult the Graduate Catalog or department office for details.
  • Master of Science (M.S.)
  • Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.)
Majors and Degrees -- undergraduate degrees link to the Degree/Academic Program Requirements Reports (APRRs).
  • Environmental Science (B.E.S.)
  • Soil and Water Science (B.S.)
    • B.S. Options:
      • Soil Science Option
      • Crop Production Option
  • Soil, Water, and Environmental Science (M.S., Ph.D.)
Undergraduate Minors -- minors link to Minor Requirements Reports.

The undergraduate programs do not require a minor; however, students may select an optional minor from the list of approved minors. Minors available within the department are:

  • Environmental Sciences Minor
  • Soil and Water Science Minor

The Department of Soil, Water and Environmental Science provides students with a variety of programs in environmental science, soil science, water quality and soil-plant-nutrient-water relationships.

For more information contact the department office listed above.
