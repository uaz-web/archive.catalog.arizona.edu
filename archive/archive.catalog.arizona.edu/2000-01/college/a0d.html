  • Pre-Dance

Undergraduate Minors -- minors link to Minor Requirement Reports.

A minor is required in the Bachelor of Arts degree programs. The minor is usually completed in a department related to the major; students are urged to consult with the major advisor.  A minor is not required for all B.F.A. and B.M. programs. Consult with your major advisor or view the APRR for your major for more information.

Minors available within the college are:

  • Art History Minor
  • Dance Minor
  • Media Arts Minor
  • Music Minor
  • Music Education Teaching Minor
  • Studio Art Minor
  • Theatre Arts Minor
  • Theatre Arts Teaching Minor
