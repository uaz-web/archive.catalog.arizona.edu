Faculty Members

For contact information see the UA Phonebook.

All permanent, tenure track faculty, multi-year lecturers, emeritus faculty, and faculty on continuing status are listed in the Catalog alphabetically by last name, including:

  • their year of Catalog eligibility (year they became a voting member of the Faculty Senate)
  • their titles and budgeted departments
  • their degrees received, with degree year and institution name
  • this information is also available in the Catalog by department and college; alternatively the academic units often post their faculty biographies on their home pages (for links to home pages see the Catalog department pages)
