Upper-Division Writing Proficiency Examination (UDWPE)
(Policy updated:  November 19, 2002) 

The Mid-Career Writing Assessment (MCWA) has replaced the former Upper-Division Writing Proficiency Exam (UDWPE) as the University writing proficiency requirement. Students who have previously satisfied the UDWPE requirement need take no further action.
