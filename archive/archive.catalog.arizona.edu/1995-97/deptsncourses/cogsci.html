Cognitive science is the interdisciplinary study of the workings of the mind. It seeks to link theories of human mental capacities to experimental and computational approaches to cognition, and to discover the ways in which the brain carries out high-level mental functions.

The program offers a Doctor of Philosophy minor in cognitive science. For admission and degree requirements, please see the Graduate Catalog.
