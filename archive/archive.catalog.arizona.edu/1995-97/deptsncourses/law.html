The College of Law offers course work leading to the Juris Doctor degree and the Master of Laws in International Trade, and it participates in several joint degree programs. Courses leading to the Juris Doctor degree are numbered at the 600-level. For a description of College of Law courses and degree requirements, please see the College of Law Catalog. The LL.M in International Trade may be earned by a limited number of students. For degree requirements, write David A. Gantz, Director of Graduate Studies, College of Law, University of Arizona, 1201 E. Speedway Blvd., Tucson, AZ 85721. The 500-level courses below identify law-related courses offered by other departments that are cross-listed with law. They may be taken to support special student interests or as part of a program for students seeking joint degrees. For information on joint degree programs, consult the College of Law and the departments offering the joint degrees with the College of Law.
