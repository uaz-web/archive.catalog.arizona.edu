Note: The Criminal Justice Administration Minor, and Health & Human Services Minor were disestablished, effective June 2009. The Public Management & Policy Minor was transferred to the College of Social & Behavioral Sciences, effective June 2009. Students interested in one of the disestablished minors are encouraged to consult with their major advisor about other options.

The School:

The School of Public Administration and Policy is administered by the Eller College of Management.

The school offers degrees designed to prepare men and women for a variety of staff and managerial positions in public sector and not-for-profit organizations and private organizations dealing with the public sector.

The School participates in the Honors program.

For more information contact the school office listed above.
