Introduction:

The College of Nursing offers a Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.S.N.) for traditional students and a 14-month accelerated B.S.N. program for college graduates.  The College also offers a Master of Science (M.S.) a Doctor of Nursing Practice (D.N.P.) and a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) with a major in Nursing.  Details regarding the College of Nursing academic programs can be found on the College web site.

The College of Nursing also administers the Graduate Program in Gerontology.

Additional information about the college is also available on the College web site.

For more information contact the college office listed above.
