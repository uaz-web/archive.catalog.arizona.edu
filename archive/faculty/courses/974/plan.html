110. Regional Land Use (3) Problems of regional environments in relation to the use and development of activities on the land. Emphasis on field study of actual land uses and introduction to the analysis and mapping of these using microcomputers. The relation of land use to taxation, zoning, and real estate transfer and development. (Identical with GEOG 110).
