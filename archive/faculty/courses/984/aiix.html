Internship (AII )

280A. Service Learning: Theory and Practice (2) I II This course introduces students to the theory behind service learning, provides a forum for discussing civic responsibility, and provides students with opportunities to participate in and reflect upon various community service projects. P, consult AIC instructor.

280B. Service Learning Internship (1-6) [Rpt./ 6 units] I II An internship combining community service, guided reflection and an academic component supervised by a faculty member. P, consult AIC instructor. Change course title to: Service Learning Experience. Effective Spring 1999.

380. Career Internship (1-6) [Rpt./ 6 units] I II A carefully monitored work experience combined with academic objectives and an academic project intended to allow students to explore a potential career, develop specific skills relevant to one or more possible careers, make contacts, etc. P, consult AIC instructor. Change course title and description to: Career Experience. A carefully monitored work experience combined with specific learning objectives and an academic project, intended to allow students to explore a potential career, develop specific skills, make contacts, etc.   Effective Spring 1999.

a. Service Learning (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II P, consult AIC instructor.
