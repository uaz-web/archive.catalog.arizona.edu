The School of Architecture is in the College of Architecture, Planning and Landscape Architecture. The School offers an accredited five-year program leading to the professional degree, Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch.). A post-professional master's program (M.Arch.) and a two-track program leading to a professional Master of Architecture degree are also offered. The School also contributes to the Bachelor of Science in Sustainable Built Environments (B.S.B.S.E.) offered by the College of Architecture and Landscape Architecture.
