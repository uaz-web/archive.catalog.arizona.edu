Academic Policies

Double Use of Courses (Double Dipping)

The use of courses to satisfy more than one requirement in the student's degree program (e.g., for a major requirement and the minor; for a General Education requirement and the minor, etc.) is subject to University General Education, college, and department policies on the double use of a course.  See the General Education policy and check with your major AND minor advisors for clarification on the double use of any courses within your primary and secondary degree program and the double use of any courses between two degree programs.
