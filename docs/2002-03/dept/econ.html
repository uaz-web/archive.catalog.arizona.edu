The Department:

The Department of Economics is in the Eller College of Business and Public Administration.

The study of economics is designed for those who wish to concentrate in economic analysis to prepare for careers in business, government, teaching, or private research and consulting.

The department participates in the honors program. Honors courses are open to both majors and non-majors.

For more information contact the department office listed above.
