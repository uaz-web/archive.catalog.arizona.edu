The School of Government and Public Policy is administered by the College of Social and Behavioral Sciences.

The school offers courses on the philosophies, processes, organizations, methods, and policies of government and related political institutions. These courses focus on government and politics in the United States and foreign countries and also on how governments of different nations relate to one another. Political science instruction is useful for pursuing careers in government, politics, law, business, education, journalism, and the media.

The school offers degrees designed to prepare students for a variety of staff and managerial positions in public sector and not-for-profit organizations and private organizations dealing with the public sector.
