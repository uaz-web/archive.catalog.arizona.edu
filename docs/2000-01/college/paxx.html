The School of Public Administration and Policy is administered by the Eller College of Business and Public Administration.

Degrees, Majors, Minors and Options:

Baccalaureate Degree

  • Bachelor of Science in Public Administration (B.S.P.A.)

Graduate Degree -- consult the Graduate Catalog or department office for details.

  • Master of Public Administration (M.P.A.)

Major and Degrees -- degrees link to the Degree/Academic Program Requirements Reports (APRRs).

  • Public Administration (M.P.A.)
  • Public Management (B.S.P.A.)
  • Health and Human Services Administration (B.S.P.A.)
  • Criminal Justice Administration (B.S.P.A.)

Undergraduate Minors -- minors link to the Minor Requirements Reports

A minor is not required for the undergraduate programs; however, students may select an optional minor from the list of approved minors.  Minors available within the school are:

  • Criminal Justice Administration
  • Health and Human Services Administration
  • Public Management

The School of Public Administration and Policy offers degrees designed to prepare men and women for a variety of staff and managerial positions in public sector and not-for-profit organizations and private organizations dealing with the public sector.

The School participates in the Honors program.

For more information contact the school office listed above.
