Summer 2001 Course Descriptions
Key to Course Descriptions

Courses offered in Summer 2001 are linked to the Schedule of Classes.

All courses listed below are approved to be taught as of  Summer 2001; however, some may not be offered this Summer.  The courses that ARE offered this Summer, in either Pre-Session, Summer I or Summer II, are linked to the Schedule of Classes.  The complete list below is a good indicator of what may be offered over the next few years (contact department about future offerings).

Insect Science (INSC)  Dept Info - College Info

INSC 404 -- Physiological Systems (3) II Writing Emphasis Course. P, satisfaction of the upper-division writing proficiency requirement; ECOL 182, MCB 181R, MCB 181L, course in biochemistry suggested. (Identical with ECOL 404, which is home).

INSC 411 -- Insect Behavior (4) I II (Identical with ENTO 411, which is home). May be convened with INSC 511.

INSC 424 -- Theoretical Population Genetics (3) I P, ECOL 320, MATH 223. (Identical with ECOL 424, which is home). May be convened with INSC 524.

INSC 470 -- Parasitoid Ecology and Biological Control (3) I P, ECOL 444 or equivalent. (Identical with ENTO 470, which is home). May be convened with INSC 570.

INSC 500A -- Topics in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology (2-3) I (Identical with ECOL 500A, which is home).

INSC 500B -- Topics in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology (3) II (Identical with ECOL 500B, which is home).

INSC 503L -- Parasitology Laboratory (1) I (Identical with V SC 503L, which is home).

INSC 503R -- Biology of Animal Parasites (3) I (Identical with V SC 503R, which is home).

INSC 505 -- Aquatic Entomology (4) II (Identical with ENTO 505, which is home).

INSC 507 -- Insect Physiology (3) II P, or CR, ENTO 407L; biochemistry recommended. (Identical with ENTO 507, which is home).

INSC 508 -- Insect Toxicology (3) II P, 3 units of organic chemistry or biochemistry. (Identical with ENTO 508, which is home).

INSC 511 -- Insect Behavior (4) I II (Identical with ENTO 511, which is home). May be convened with INSC 411.

INSC 515L -- Insect Biology Laboratory (1) I P, ECOL 182. (Identical with ENTO 515L, which is home).

INSC 515R -- Insect Biology (3) I P, ECOL 182. (Identical with ENTO 515R, which is home).

INSC 517 -- Insect Systematics (4) I 3R, 3L. (Identical with ENTO 517, which is home).

INSC 518 -- Laboratory Methods in Insect Physiology (3) II Modern methods of laboratory investigations of the physiology, neurobiology, toxicology, biochemistry, and the molecular biology of insects. P, ENTO 515, biochemistry is preferred. (Identical with ECOL 518, ENTO 518, BIOC 518).

INSC 524 -- Theoretical Population Genetics (3) I (Identical with ECOL 524, which is home). May be convened with INSC 424.

INSC 544 -- Insect Ecology (3) I (Identical with ENTO 544, which is home).

INSC 545 -- Concepts in Genetic Analysis (3) I P, introductory undergraduate genetics course or biology course. (Identical with MCB 545, which is home).

INSC 552 -- Medical Veterinary Entomology (4) [Rpt./ 3] II (Identical with ENTO 552, which is home).

INSC 568 -- Nucleic Acids (4) I P, BIOC 411 or BIOC 51l, consent of instructor. (Identical with BIOC 568, which is home).

INSC 570 -- Parasitoid Ecology and Biological Control (3) I (Identical with ENTO 570, which is home). May be convened with INSC 470.

INSC 588 -- Principles of Cellular and Molecular Neurobiology (4) I P, consult program office before enrolling. (Identical with NRSC 588, which is home).

INSC 589 -- Principles of Systems Neurobiology (4) II P, NRSC 588, consult program office before enrolling. (Identical with NRSC 589, which is home).

INSC 599 -- Independent Study (1-4) Contact department for a description of this course.

INSC 613 -- Applied Biostatistics (4) II 3R, 3L. (Identical with RNR 613, which is home).

INSC 700 -- Methods in Insect Science (3) [Rpt./ 3] Research rotations in the laboratories of faculty members within the Insect Science program. May not be repeated with the same faculty member. P, consult department before enrolling. Open to majors only.

INSC 900 -- Research (1-8) [Rpt.] I II Contact department for a description of this course.

INSC 920 -- Dissertation (1-9) [Rpt.] I II Contact department for a description of this course.

INSC 930 -- Supplementary Registration (1-9) [Rpt.] I II Contact department for a description of this course.
