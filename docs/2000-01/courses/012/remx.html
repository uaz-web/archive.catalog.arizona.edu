Courses offered in Summer 2001 are linked to the Schedule of Classes.

All courses listed below are approved to be taught as of  Summer 2001; however, some may not be offered this Summer.  The courses that ARE offered this Summer, in either Pre-Session, Summer I or Summer II, are linked to the Schedule of Classes.  The complete list below is a good indicator of what may be offered over the next few years (contact department about future offerings).

Remote Sensing (REM )  Dept Info - College Info

REM 490 -- Remote Sensing for the Study of Planet Earth (3) II A multidisciplinary course delineating the physical basis of electromagnetic remote sensing, the concepts of information extraction, and applications pertinent to earth systems science. (Identical with G EN 490, OPTI 490, SWES 490, ATMO 490, RNR 490, HWR 490, GEOS 490). May be convened with REM 590.

REM 590 -- Remote Sensing for the Study of Planet Earth (3) II For a description of course topics see REM 490. Graduate-level requirements include an additional term paper. (Identical with HWR 590, OPTI 590, GEOS 590, ATMO 590, MN E 590, ARL 590, SWES 590, RNR 590). May be convened with REM 490.

REM 696A -- Remote Sensing (1) II Contact department for a description of this course.
