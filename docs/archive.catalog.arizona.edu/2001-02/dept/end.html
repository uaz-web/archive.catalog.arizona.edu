Undergraduate Minors

The college does not require an undergraduate minor; however, students may select an optional minor.  Minors available within the college are:

  • Aerospace Engineering Minor
  • Agricultural and Biosystems Engineering Minor1
  • Chemical Engineering Minor
  • Civil Engineering Minor
  • Computer Engineering Minor
  • Electrical Engineering Minor
  • Environmental Hydrology and Water Resources Minor
  • Geological Engineering Minor
  • Materials Science and Engineering Minor
  • Mechanical Engineering Minor
  • Mining Engineering Minor
  • Systems and Industrial Engineering Minor

1Jointly administered with the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences
