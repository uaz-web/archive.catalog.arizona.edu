Degrees, Majors, Minors and Options:

Baccalaureate Degrees

The program does not offer a baccalaureate degree; however it does offer a concentration for the interdisciplinary studies (IDS) major.

Undergraduate Minors -- minors link to Minor Requirements Reports.

Minors available within the department are:

  • Humanities Minor

The humanities program provides interdisciplinary courses and programs that focus on human achievements as expressed in works of art, literature, religion, philosophy, and science. Courses are designed to teach students various aspects of distinctive cultural heritages, and to make them aware of ethical and aesthetic concerns raised by such studies so that students, as citizens of the future, may participate in local and world communities with a clear sense of responsibility and purpose.

For more information contact the program office listed above.
