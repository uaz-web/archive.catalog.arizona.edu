The School of Information Resources and Library Science is in the College of Social and Behavioral Sciences.

The School offers courses focusing on the study of information and its use as a social phenomenon.  The school offers an undergraduate minor, the Master of Arts degree with a major in library science and the Doctor of Philosophy degree with a major in library science.

For more information contact the school office listed above.
