Winter 04/Spring 05 Course Descriptions

All courses below are approved to be taught in Winter 2004 and Spring 2005; however, some (or all) may not be offered in either term.  The courses that are offered in Spring link to the Schedule of Classes.  Classes with alternative delivery modes (Web based, cable TV, correspondence, etc) are noted in the Schedule at the section level.  The complete list below is a good indicator of what may be offered over the next few years (contact department about offerings).  For explanations of course elements see the Key to Course Descriptions.

Italian (ITAL)  Department Info

ITAL 101 -- Elementary Italian I  (4 units)
Description:  Listening, speaking, reading, and writing; introduction to the basic structures and vocabulary of Italian. (Does not count toward the Italian major or minor.)
Grading:  Regular grades are awarded for this course: A B C D E. Available to qualified students for Pass/Fail Option.
Special exam:  course may be taken by special exam for credit (not for grade).
Usually offered:  Fall, Spring, Summer.

ITAL 102 -- Elementary Italian II  (4 units)
Description:  Listening, speaking, reading and writing; an introduction to the basic structures and vocabulary of Italian, continuation. (Does not count toward the Italian major or minor.)
Grading:  Regular grades are awarded for this course: A B C D E. Available to qualified students for Pass/Fail Option.
Prerequisite(s):  ITAL 101 or placement.
Special exam:  course may be taken by special exam for credit (not for grade).
Usually offered:  Fall, Spring.

ITAL 102Z -- Intensive Elementary Italian  (4 units)
Description:  Course represents an accelerated course covering the material of both 101 and 102 (and not merely an accelerated 102 class) therefore, it is not recommended for those students who have already taken 101. Does not count toward the Italian major or minor.
Grading:  Regular grades are awarded for this course: A B C D E.
Prerequisite(s):  language major or demonstrated language proficiency.
Usually offered:  Fall.
