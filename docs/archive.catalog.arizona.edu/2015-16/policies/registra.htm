Academic Policies

Class Registration

All persons must register and pay tuition and fees in order to attend class.  Complete registration instructions, procedures, and deadlines for which every student is fully responsible are detailed in the Schedule of Classes' Registration Information section.

Priority Registration periods, based primarily on class standing, are designated by the Registrar for Fall and Spring semesters.

The term-specific class offerings/availability are in the Schedule of Classes about 3 weeks prior to the priority registration periods.

A student is officially registered and eligible to attend classes only when all procedures have been completed, including payment of tuition and fees.
