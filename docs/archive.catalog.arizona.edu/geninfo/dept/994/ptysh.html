PLANETARY SCIENCES (PTYS)

The department offers a multidisciplinary program leading to the Master of Science in Planetary Sciences and the Doctor of Philosophy in Planetary Sciences. Also offered are both undergraduate and graduate minors.

For admission and degree requirements, please see the Graduate Catalog or the department's website at http://www.lpl.arizona.edu.
