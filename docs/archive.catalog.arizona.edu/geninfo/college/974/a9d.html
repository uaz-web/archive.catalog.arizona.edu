The Interdisciplinary Studies (IDS) Program

The IDS major is an alternative to a traditional major/minor program, allowing a student to design a program that reflects unique educational interests and career goals. During the sophomore or junior year, students submit proposals to University College outlining their intended course of study. For more information about the program and application process, see the IDS entry under "College Information", or call 621-7763.
