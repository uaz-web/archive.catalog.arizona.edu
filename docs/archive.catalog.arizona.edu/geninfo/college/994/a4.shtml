The College of Humanities offers programs dedicated to literacy, language-learning, and cross-cultural understanding. The college includes several interdisciplinary programs, and offers degrees in over thirteen languages. It also supports special emphases in creative writing, English as a second language, religious studies, comparative cultural and literary studies, and classical archaeology, as well as public programs offered by the Poetry Center and the Humanities Seminars.
