The College of Science develops new knowledge about the world and about means of inquiry in significant areas of science as well as interdisciplinary areas involving science. It provides students, colleagues and the public with an understanding of the history, findings, applications, and methods of scientific inquiry they need to work and participate effectively in resolving the issues of our time.
