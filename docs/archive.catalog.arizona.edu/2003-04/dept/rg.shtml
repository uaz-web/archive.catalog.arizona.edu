The College:

The Graduate College administers all University of Arizona graduate degrees. The college publishes the Graduate Catalog which contains information about available graduate programs, admissions and other college policies.  For more information about a particular graduate program, consult the Graduate Catalog or the offering department.

Graduate Interdisciplinary Programs:

Graduate Interdisciplinary Programs (GIDPs) are a hallmark of the University of Arizona and an integral part of the University's mission.  By crossing departmental boundaries, GIDPs are on the cutting edge of the fusion of ideas, techniques, and expertise into modern and imaginative methods of research and the creation of new fields of endeavor. GIDPs provide students with an interdisciplinary foundation to meet the evolving and varied demands of the future. The GIDPs are one of the special educational and research aspects of this University that give us the reputation of being a leader in academic innovation. 

For more information about GIDPs please contact the Graduate Interdisciplinary Programs Administration Office or the GIDP directly (see below).
