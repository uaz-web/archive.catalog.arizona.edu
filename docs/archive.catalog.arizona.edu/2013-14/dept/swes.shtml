The Department of Soil, Water, and Environmental Science is in the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences. The Department is also in the School of Earth and Environmental Sciences in the College of Science.

The Department provides students with a variety of programs in environmental science, soil science, water quality and soil-plant-nutrient-water relationships.
