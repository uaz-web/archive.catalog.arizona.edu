The Department:

The Department of Pharmacology is in the College of Medicine, and the Pharmacology and Toxicology Graduate Interdisciplinary Program is administered by the Graduate College.

Faculty from the Department of Pharmacology participate in the Pharmacology and Toxicology GIDP, which offers both the M.S. and Ph.D. in pharmacology and toxicology. Information about those degree programs is available in the pharmacology and toxicology section.

For more information contact the department office listed above.
