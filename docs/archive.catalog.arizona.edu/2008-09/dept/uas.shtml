General Information:
The University of Arizona South provides quality liberal arts and professional instruction as a branch campus of the University of Arizona. UA South offers both resident and distance learning modalities to provide options for students throughout southern Arizona. Offices and classrooms are located at the Sierra Vista Campus, the Douglas Office, the Pima Community College East Campus, and the Vail School District Main Office.

 

Faculty: Faculty Members are listed on the UA South site.

Degrees, Majors, Minors, Options and Certificates:
UA South offers three unique undergraduate programs, as well as degree programs that are shared with the main campus in Tucson.  UA South degree program options vary by location. All degrees listed below are available through the Sierra Vista Campus. Those marked “*” are also available through the Pima Community College East Campus.


NOTE: Main campus students may be able to take classes through UA South and apply them to certain majors and degrees offered at the main campus. Students should check with their major advisor to verify that courses taken through UA South will meet specific UA main campus degree requirements.

 

When registering for a UA South Course please be sure to note the location of the course offering.
