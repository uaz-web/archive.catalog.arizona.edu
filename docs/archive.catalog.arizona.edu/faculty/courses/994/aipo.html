Arizona International College: Language and Culture (Portuguese) (AIPO)  College Info

AIPO 105. Beginning Intensive Portuguese (5) I II Intensive course designed for students with previous experience with another Romance language. Focuses on the four skills of speaking, reading, writing and listening in Portuguese in the cultural context of Brazil. Courses are taught with the communicative approach. Students will access WWW sites in Brazil and will touch upon the history and culture of other Lusophone regions of the world. P, consent of instructor.

AIPO 205. Intermediate Intensive Portuguese (5) I II Intensive course designed for students with previous experience with another Romance language. Focuses on the four skills of speaking, reading, writing and listening in Portuguese in the cultural context of Brazil. Courses are taught with the Communicative approach.
