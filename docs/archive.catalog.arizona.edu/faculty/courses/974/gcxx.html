GLOBAL CHANGE (GC)

572. Global Biogeochemical Cycles (3) Study of processes affecting global chemical fluxes. Particular attention to current global concerns, i.e., ozone hole, carbon cycle, climate warming, atmospheric oxidation, hydrologic cycle. (Identical with GEOS 572 and HWR 572).
