The interdisciplinary Committee on Nutritional Sciences administers a campus-wide, interdisciplinary program which includes faculty members from the colleges of Agriculture, Arts and Sciences, and Medicine.

The committee offers graduate work leading to the Doctor of Philosophy degree with a major in nutritional sciences. Options in nutritional biochemistry, food safety, human nutrition (clinical or community), or animal nutrition may be selected within this major. For admission and degree requirements, please see the Graduate Catalog.
