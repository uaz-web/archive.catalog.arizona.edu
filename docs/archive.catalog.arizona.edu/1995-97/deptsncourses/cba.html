Research areas of faculty include cell biology, developmental biology, endocrinology, molecular biology, neurobiology, reproductive biology, systems biology and biological anthropology. The Department of Cell Biology and Anatomy offers a program of study leading up to the Doctor of Philosophy degree. The Master of Science is offered only in rare instances in which students are unable to continue in the doctoral program. For admission and degree requirements, please see the Graduate Catalog.
