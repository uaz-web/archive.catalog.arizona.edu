The degrees offered are the Bachelor of Science in Nursing, Master of Science, and Doctor of Philosophy with a major in nursing. For undergraduate admission and degree requirements, please see the College of Nursing section of this catalog. For graduate admission and degree requirements, please see the Graduate Catalog.

Starting with the nursing major courses in the sophomore year, all baccalaureate nursing students are required to provide their own transportation to the clinical areas where they are assigned for patient-care experience. Baccalaureate students wear an official College of Nursing uniform for clinical courses. During these semesters the student must be enrolled for all required courses.

The College participates in the university honors program.
