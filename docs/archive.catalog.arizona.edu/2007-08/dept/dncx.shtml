Introduction:

The  School of Dance  is in the College of Fine Arts.

The School of Dance offers a vigorous curriculum with equal emphasis in ballet, modern, and jazz technique.  Dance history, music, improvisation, composition, methodology, anatomy and injury prevention are also essential to both the BFA and MFA degree programs.  Choreography and performance are continually evident with numerous public performances at the award winning Stevie Eller Dance Theater.

For more information contact the department office listed above.
