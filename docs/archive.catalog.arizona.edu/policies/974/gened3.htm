General Education:
Tier One Courses that Satisfy 1997-98 Study Area Requirements

Many courses that satisfied 1997-1998 General Education Requirements are no longer offered. Those course that have equivalents available as Tier One courses under the new 1998-1999 general education program are listed below. Continuing students may use the Tier One equivalent of a general education course to satisfy 1997-98 requirements, providing there are seats available. Consult an academic advisor for more information.
