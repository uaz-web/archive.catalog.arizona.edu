Change Of Major Or College

A student may change his or her major by contacting his or her college dean and completing the appropriate forms. Students wishing to change colleges must consult the dean's office of the college to which they wish to transfer. Change from one college to another is established by filing a change of college form with the new college. The change of college will be effective for the current term if filed within the first four weeks of classes during a regular semester. If filed after that date, the change of college will be effective the following semester.
