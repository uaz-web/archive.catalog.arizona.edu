Change Of Registration From Credit To Audit

After the fourth week of classes, a change in registration in a course from credit to audit will be permitted only if the student is doing passing work in that course and receives the approval of the course instructor. After the eighth week of classes, changes from credit to audit will be permitted only with permission from the student's college dean.
