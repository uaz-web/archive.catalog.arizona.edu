CRITICAL LANGUAGES (CRL)

101. Elementary Language Study (4) [Rpt./ ] Introduction to the language with an emphasis on its spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly tutorial reviews. 2R, 6L. P, course may be repeated if language is different. Fee.

102. Elementary Language Study (4) [Rpt./ ] Continued introduction to the language with an emphasis on its spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly tutorial reviews. P, CRL 101. Course may be repeated if language is different. Fee.

197. Workshop

a. Elementary Language Study (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II P, CRL 102 or consent of CLP/language examiner. Fee.

201. Intermediate Language Study (4) [Rpt./ ] Continuing study of the language with emphasis on its spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly reviews. 2R, 6L. P, CRL 102. Course may be repeated if language is different. Fee.

202. Intermediate Language Study (4) [Rpt./ ] Continuing study of the language with an emphasis on its spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly reviews. 2R, 6L. P, CRL 201. Course may be repeated if language is different. Fee.

297. Workshop

a. Intermediate Language Study (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II P, CRL 202 or consent of CLP/language examiner. Fee.

301. Advanced Language Study (3) [Rpt./] I II Continuing study of the language with an emphasis on developing reading and writing skills, continuing practice with spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly reviews. 2R, 4L. P, proficiency at 202 level; course may be repeated if language is different. Fee.

302. Advanced Language Study (3) [Rpt./ ] I II Continuing study of the language with an emphasis on developing reading and writing skills, continuing practice with spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly reviews. 2R, 4L. P, proficiency at 202 level. Course may be repeated if language is different. Fee.

397. Workshop

a. Advanced Language Study (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II P, CRL 302 or consent of CLP/language examiner. Fees.

497. Workshop

a. Specialized Language Study (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II P, CRL 397 or consent of language examiner. Fees.
