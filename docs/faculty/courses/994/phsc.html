PHSC 513. Health Technology Assessment (3) II Provides an overview of methods in health technology assessment, including cost-benefit, cost-effectiveness, and cost-utility analysis, Current controversies in technology assessment are discussed. Emphasis will be placed on critical assessment of the published literature. P, PHSC 511. Open to majors only. (Identical with PHL 513).
