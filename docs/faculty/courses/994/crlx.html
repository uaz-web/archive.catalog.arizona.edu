Critical Languages (CRL )  Dept Info - College Info

CRL 101. Elementary Language Study (4) [Rpt./ 1] Introduction to the language with an emphasis on its spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly tutorial reviews. 2R, 6L. P, course may be repeated if language is different.

CRL 102. Elementary Language Study (4) [Rpt./ 1] Continued introduction to the language with an emphasis on its spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly tutorial reviews. P, CRL 101. Course may be repeated if language is different.

CRL 197A. Elementary Language Study (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II P, CRL 102 or consent of CLP/language examiner.

CRL 201. Intermediate Language Study (4) [Rpt./ 1] Continuing study of the language with emphasis on its spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly reviews. 2R, 6L. P, CRL 102. Course may be repeated if language is different.

CRL 202. Intermediate Language Study (4) [Rpt./ 1] Continuing study of the language with an emphasis on its spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly reviews. 2R, 6L. P, CRL 201. Course may be repeated if language is different.

CRL 297A. Intermediate Language Study (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II P, CRL 202 or consent of CLP/language examiner.

CRL 301. Advanced Language Study (3) [Rpt./ 1] I II Continuing study of the language with an emphasis on developing reading and writing skills, continuing practice with spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly reviews. 2R, 4L. P, proficiency at 202 level; course may be repeated if language is different.

CRL 302. Advanced Language Study (3) [Rpt./ 1] I II Continuing study of the language with an emphasis on developing reading and writing skills, continuing practice with spoken aspects utilizing tape-intensive preparations with biweekly reviews. 2R, 4L. P, proficiency at 202 level. Course may be repeated if language is different.

CRL 397A. Advanced Language Study (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II P, CRL 302 or consent of CLP/language examiner.

CRL 497A. Specialized Language Study (1-6) [Rpt./ 12 units] I II P, CRL 397 or consent of language examiner.
