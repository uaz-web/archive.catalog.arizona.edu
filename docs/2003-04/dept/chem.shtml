  • Chemistry Minor

The Department:

The Department of Chemistry is in the College of Science.

The department provides both general and professional training, giving a strong foundation upon which to base a career in the fields of medicine and related health sciences, in secondary education, or leading to industrial work or graduate specialization in chemistry.

The department participates in the honors program.

For more information contact the department office listed above.
