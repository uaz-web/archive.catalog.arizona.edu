Undergraduate Minors

Most undergraduate programs in the College of Science require a minor. Students may select any minor from among the list of approved University minors. Minors available within the college are:

Astronomy

Atmospheric Sciences

Biochemistry

Chemistry

Chemistry Teaching Minor

Computer Science

Computer Science Teaching Minor

Earth Science Teaching Minor

Ecology and Evolutionary Biology

General Biology

General Biology Teaching Minor

Geosciences

Mathematics

Molecular and Cellular Biology

Physics

Physics Teaching Minor

Planetary Sciences

Speech & Hearing Sciences

*The Master of Science degree with a major in Biochemistry is awarded only in rare instances when individuals admitted to the Doctor of Philosophy degree are unable to continue in the doctoral program.

** The General Biology major is administered by the department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology.

***Administered by the department of Veterinary Science and Microbiology in the College of Agriculture.
