Associate Teaching Majors

The College of Fine Arts, the College of Agriculture, and the School of Health Related Professions offer programs for training teachers in their particular disciplines in association with the College of Education.

Major and Degree: Academic Unit:
Agricultural Education (B.S.A.)* College of Agriculture
Art Education (B.F.A.) College of Fine Arts
Family and Consumer Sciences Ed. (B.S.F.)* School of Family & Consumer Resources
Health Education (B.S.H.S.) School of Health Professions
Music Education (B.M.) Instrumental Voice School of Music & Dance
Theatre Arts Education (B.F.A.) College of Fine Arts

* Effective Fall 2000, all B.S.A. and B.S.F. degrees have been renamed B.S. degrees.

Students in the programs listed above earn degrees specific to the units. Refer to the administering unit for more information.
