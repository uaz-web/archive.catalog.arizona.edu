The study of human beings, individually and in social groups, unites the departments and programs of the College of Social and Behavioral Sciences. The college promotes fundamental research in individual behavior, cultural expression, social organization, theory and values, and public and private policy. The departments and programs provide bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees, with majors and minors in a number of disciplinary and interdisciplinary areas.
