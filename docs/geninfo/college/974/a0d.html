Undergraduate Minors

A minor is required in the Bachelor of Arts degree programs. The minor is usually completed in a department related to the major and must be approved by the major advisor.

The Minor for B.F.A. and B.M. Programs

A minor is not required for all B.F.A. and B.M. programs.
