    Sociology

    Theatre Arts Education

    Majors in elementary education require an academic concentration, chosen in consultation with an academic advisor from the Office of Student and Career Services (621-7865). The non-teaching major in special education and rehabilitation requires a minor chosen from the following disciplines:

    Anthropology

    Psychology

    Sociology

    Special Education

    (other minors may be approved by the department.)

    For specific information on the majors and minors available for each of the other degrees offered by the college, please consult department sections of the on-line catalog, or contact the Office of Student and Career Services (621-7865) in the College of Education.
