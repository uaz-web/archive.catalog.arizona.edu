The School of Health Professions educates students for careers in health professions in the university, community, and commercial sectors. Its programs offer science-based preparation for researchers, technicians and teachers who will advance knowledge of human health, prevent disease, and enhance physical performance. The school also offers courses for graduate and professional study in the health science fields.
