The School:

The School of Landscape Architecture and Planning is in the College of Architecture and Landscape Architecture.

Landscape Architecture MLA:

The Master of Landscape Architecture is a graduate professional degree program which emphasizes landscape and human ecology, socio-cultural and behavioral factors, landscape aesthetics, and artistic principles in a variety of design, planning, and management scenarios.
