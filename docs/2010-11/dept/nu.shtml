Introduction:

The College of Nursing offers a Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.S.N.) for traditional students and a 14-month accelerated B.S.N. program for college graduates. Students in the Pre-Nursing Program at the University of Arizona are enrolled in the Colleges of Letters, Arts, and Science.

At the graduate level, the College offers a Master of Science (M.S.) a Doctor of Nursing Practice (D.N.P.) and a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) with a major in Nursing.  Details regarding the College of Nursing academic programs can be found on the College web site.

Additional information about the college is also available on the College web site.

For more information contact the college office listed above.
