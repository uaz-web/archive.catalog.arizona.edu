The Program:

The Applied Biosciences GIDP is administered by the Graduate Interdisciplinary Programs Office.

This interdisciplinary Professional Science Master’s Program allows students to combine the life sciences with training in business and management.  It is designed for students seeking careers in industry, commerce, and government. Students take core biosciences courses, electives from a variety of departments, and business and project management courses. Students also complete an internship in industry. 

Students may specialize in any area of the life sciences using the resources of the biological sciences departments in different UA colleges on the main campus and the Arizona Health Sciences Center.  The research laboratories at the University promote collaborative efforts among many departments.  As a result, students have the opportunity to incorporate different areas of the life sciences into their program, creating an individualized plan of study reflecting their personal interests and career goals.

For more information about graduate admission criteria and available programs contact the program office.
