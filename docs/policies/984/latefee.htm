Late Payment And Late Registration Fees

A student who fails to complete payment of all fees prior to the due date for any semester or term will be assessed a non-refundable late payment fee. Students who fail to register prior to the first day of class will be assessed an additional late registration fee. For a schedule of these fees, see Late Penalties in the Schedule of Classes.
