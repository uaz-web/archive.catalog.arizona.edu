Academic Policies

This section includes current university-wide academic policies.  Additional academic policies related to specific degree programs and majors can be found by consulting information provided by the offering unit (link to academic unit home pages from the Catalog's college and department pages).  Graduate students are advised to consult the Graduate Catalog for additional graduate level policy.
