The School of Middle Eastern and North African Studies is in the College of Social and Behavioral Sciences.

Middle Eastern and North African Studies investigates the history, cultures, languages and geography of the region. This region includes the Arab world, Turkey, Iran and Israel, and ranges from Morocco on the Atlantic Ocean to the Persian Gulf.
