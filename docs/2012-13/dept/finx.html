The Department of Finance is in the Eller College of Management.

Students majoring in finance are prepared for corporate financial management, investment portfolio management, brokerage, and investment and commercial banking.

Masters Programs

Graduate courses in finance can be taken in connection with the M.S. program in management.  Consult the Finance department for details.  

The department also participates in the M.B.A. program, available through the Eller Graduate School of Management.

Ph.D. Program

Graduate courses in finance can be taken in connection with the Ph.D. program in management. Consult the Finance department office for details.

B.S.B.A. Program

Undergraduate majors in finance are offered through the Bachelor of Science in Business Administration.  Non-business students interested in a minor in one of these areas should contact the BPA Undergraduate Office at (520) 621-2505.

For more information contact the department office listed above.
