The College of Law offers course work leading to the Juris Doctor
degree. The course program has been thoroughly revised and
expanded to include a modernized set of required courses and a
wide variety of problem-method courses, seminars and clinical
programs. For course descriptions and degree requirements, please
see the College of Law Catalog.
