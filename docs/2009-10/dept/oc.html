The College:

The UA Outreach College provides credit and noncredit educational opportunities to people seeking university-level instruction and enrichment outside the scope of the traditional campus experience. Providing lifetime learning across the age spectrum, UA Outreach College offers courses and programs for professional, academic, and personal advancement that help strengthen and advance the outreach mission of The University of Arizona.

For more information contact the college office listed above.
