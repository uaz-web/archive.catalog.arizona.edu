    Degrees, Majors and Minors

    The Colleges offer several programs:  a Bachelor of Arts in Interdisciplinary Studies, a Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Science in Global Studies, a Bachelor of General Studies Degree, and two pre-professional programs.  In addition, students who have not selected a major are enrolled in the Colleges.
