Introduction:

Students in the Pre-Professional Nursing program at the University of Arizona are enrolled in the College of Nursing and must apply for admission to the Professional B.S.N. program.  The College of Nursing offers a Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.S.N.) for students desiring to enter the profession. At the master degree level, the College offers two Master of Science (M.S.) degrees: a 15 month accelerated Entry to the Profession of Nursing program as well as a Registered Nursing to M.S. degree program. At the doctoral level, the College of Nursing offers a Doctor of Nursing Practice (D.N.P.) and Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) with a major in Nursing.

Details regarding the College of Nursing academic programs can be found on the College of Nursing web site.
