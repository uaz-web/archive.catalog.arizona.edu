The Department:

The Department of German Studies is in the School of International Languages, Literatures, and Cultures within the College of Humanities.

The department provides instruction designed to develop fluency in oral and written communication, knowledge of German literature, and insights into German cultural history. A number of opportunities for study and/or work abroad are available, including the department's summer program in Heidelberg.  A major in German Studies, by itself or in combination with another field, can open the door to careers in education, international business, the Foreign Service and many other professions.

For more information contact the department office listed above.
